# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is this product?
Our product is a comprehensive technical support platform designed to help teams manage and resolve issues efficiently.

### How do I get started?
Visit our website at example.com and click "Get Started". Follow the onboarding wizard to set up your account.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features.

## Account and Billing

### How much does it cost?
Pricing starts at $29/month for the starter plan. See our pricing page for all plans and features.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.

## Technical Questions

### What are the system requirements?
Our web application works on all modern browsers. For mobile, we support iOS 12+ and Android 8+.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit. All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.

### Do you backup my data?
Yes, we perform automated daily backups. Data is backed up redundantly across multiple geographic locations.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your data at any time as JSON or CSV from your account settings.

## Usage and Features

### How do I create a project?
Navigate to the Projects tab and click "Create New Project". Enter your project details and click "Create".

### Can I customize the dashboard?
Yes, you can customize your dashboard by dragging and dropping widgets, or selecting from our widget library.

### How do I set up notifications?
Go to Settings > Notifications and configure your preferences. You can choose email, SMS, or in-app notifications.

### Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes, we support integrations with Slack, Jira, GitHub, Azure DevOps, and 50+ other tools.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions to reset your password.

### Why can't I log in?
Common reasons include incorrect password, account not verified, or account suspension. Try resetting your password first.

### The app is running slowly
Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions, or trying a different browser.

### How do I report a bug?
Report bugs through your account settings > Help > Report a Bug, or email bugs@example.com with details and screenshots.
